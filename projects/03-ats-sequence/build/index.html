ATS sequence simulator

Utility fails. Sense, override, engine start, transfer, retransfer, cooldown, on a published vendor's own timers.

Educational. Not for field use. This reproduces a manufacturer's published sequence of operation so it can be explored interactively. It is not a substitute for the manufacturer's instructions, an engineered study, or a qualified person on site.
